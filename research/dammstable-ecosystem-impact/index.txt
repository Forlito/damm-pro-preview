15:T21d9,<p><em>A case study on 398 days of Uniswap v3 stablecoin market making on Arbitrum, as of September 4, 2025.</em></p>
<p>This report is aimed at showing other metrics and impacts (not solely LP profitability) — but for the record: the average return for LPs was around <strong>11.5%</strong> for the whole period.</p>
<p>We were doing it <strong>self-custodially</strong>, meaning we didn&#39;t want to accept more deposits for legal reasons. We only made it available to <strong>whitelisted investors</strong> in <strong>September 2025</strong>.</p>
<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p><a href="https://dammcap.finance/funds/dammstable">DAMMstable</a> is an algorithmic stablecoin liquidity provision fund designed to generate risk-adjusted returns while strengthening the health of decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystems. Beyond investor performance, DAMMstable plays a critical role in <strong>deepening and improving the efficiency of liquidity</strong> across stablecoin pairs.</p>
<p>This report analyzes how DAMMstable has contributed to the DeFi ecosystem over its <strong>398-day track record</strong>, initially as a custodial fund and more recently as a decentralized Lagoon fund. The fund has operated in three 0.01% fee pools on Uniswap v3:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>USDC/USDT</strong></li>
<li><strong>USDC.e/USDT</strong></li>
<li><strong>DAI/USDT</strong></li>
</ul>
<h2>Liquidity efficiency: Fee Multiplier Ratio</h2>
<p>A key measure of DAMMstable&#39;s contribution is how efficiently it provides liquidity relative to its share of pool TVL.</p>
<p><strong>Methodology:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>For every position taken, calculate the % of total pool TVL represented.</li>
<li>Compare it to the % of pool fees captured during the position&#39;s lifetime.</li>
<li>Define the <strong>Fee Multiplier Ratio (FMR)</strong> = (% Fees Captured) ÷ (% TVL).</li>
<li>A ratio &gt; 1 means the position earns a disproportionate share of fees, signaling superior capital efficiency.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Results:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Over 398 days, DAMMstable took <strong>3,370 liquidity positions</strong>.</li>
<li>Average Fee Multiplier Ratio = <strong>3.28</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<p><img src="/damm-pro-preview/research/dammstable-ecosystem-impact/fee-multiplier-ratio.jpg" alt="Fee Multiplier Ratio across 3,370 positions"></p>
<p><strong>Interpretation:</strong> DAMMstable&#39;s capital was consistently positioned in tight, high-usage ranges, earning more than <strong>3× its &quot;fair share&quot;</strong> of fees relative to TVL. This demonstrates that DAMMstable not only concentrates liquidity but also places it correctly, enhancing execution quality for traders while amplifying fee capture for LPs.</p>
<h2>Volume serviced vs. fund capital</h2>
<p>Another KPI tracks the trading activity supported by DAMMstable&#39;s liquidity.</p>
<p><strong>Methodology:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>From fees collected, deduce the underlying trade volume serviced.</li>
<li>Compare total serviced volume to DAMMstable&#39;s capital base.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Results:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Total volume serviced: <strong>$526 million</strong></li>
<li>Average daily volume serviced: <strong>$1.3 million</strong></li>
<li>Average fund TVL: <strong>$221k</strong></li>
<li>Volume/TVL ratio: <strong>5.96</strong></li>
</ul>

<h2>Capital efficiency</h2>
<p>Another critical KPI is how effectively DAMMstable converts investor capital into active, working liquidity.</p>
<p><strong>Findings since Lagoon launch:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Average allocation to Uniswap positions: <strong>99%</strong> of fund capital.</li>
<li>Capital efficiency within allocation: <strong>99.9%</strong>.</li>
</ul>
<p>This means nearly all fund capital is consistently deployed in live, minted positions, with only <strong>0.1%</strong> left idle as unutilized ERC-20 balances of token0 or token1.</p>
<p><strong>Interpretation:</strong> Such efficiency demonstrates:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Minimal idle capital</strong> — investor funds are always working.</li>
<li><strong>High utilization</strong> — liquidity is continuously active, compounding fee generation.</li>
<li><strong>Ecosystem benefit</strong> — more stable, predictable depth in the stablecoin pools DAMMstable supports.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Ecosystem impact</h2>
<p>DAMMstable&#39;s liquidity provision directly benefits the broader DeFi ecosystem by:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Deepening markets</strong> — Concentrated liquidity ensures stablecoin trades clear with minimal slippage.</li>
<li><strong>Efficient capital allocation</strong> — High Fee Multiplier Ratios show capital is strategically deployed where traders need it most.</li>
<li><strong>Sustaining decentralized infrastructure</strong> — Transition to the Lagoon fund improves transparency and accessibility.</li>
<li><strong>Maximizing capital efficiency</strong> — With 99.9% utilization, virtually all capital strengthens ecosystem liquidity.</li>
<li><strong>Enhancing user experience</strong> — Traders experience more predictable execution, while protocols relying on stablecoin liquidity (lending, leverage, derivatives) benefit from robust base pairs.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Conclusion</h2>
<p>DAMMstable is not only an algorithmic yield-generating vehicle but also an <strong>ecosystem enabler</strong>. Its <strong>3.28x fee efficiency</strong> and <strong>5.96x volume multiplier</strong> demonstrate how algorithmic liquidity provision can magnify the depth and quality of DeFi&#39;s most critical markets: stablecoins.</p>
<p>By continuing to refine its strategies and operating fully decentralized, DAMMstable sets a standard for how market-neutral funds can deliver both <strong>investor returns</strong> and <strong>public goods</strong> to the DeFi economy.</p>

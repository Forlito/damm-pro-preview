Privacy & Cookies

This Privacy Policy outlines how Damm Cap (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and processes your personal data when you use our services. We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other relevant privacy laws.

1. Data Controller

Damm Cap, operated by Damm Labs. Ltd., located at Caiman, acts as the Data Controller responsible for processing your personal data. For inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at team@dammcap.finance

2. Data We Collect

We may collect personal data when you use our services or interact with our platform. This data may include:

  • Wallet addresses and transaction data
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Communication data when you contact us
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser information

We may collect this data directly from you or through automated means, such as cookies or blockchain interactions.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following lawful grounds:

  • Contractual necessity - to provide our services
  • Legitimate interests - to improve our platform and services
  • Legal obligations - to comply with applicable laws
  • Consent - for marketing communications and analytics

4. How We Use Your Data

Your personal data is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with access to our platform and execute your transactions.
  • To maintain security, prevent fraud, and ensure the proper functioning of our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including tax and regulatory reporting.
  • To communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries and providing service updates.
  • To improve our platform by analyzing user behavior and trends.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect usage data and improve your experience on our platform. You may manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings or through the cookie consent banner on our site. Cookies that are strictly necessary for the operation of the platform are enabled by default, while others (e.g., analytics cookies) require your explicit consent.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. For example:

  • Transaction data is retained to fulfill contractual and legal obligations.
  • Data used for marketing is retained until you withdraw your consent or object to the processing.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights, please contact us at team@dammcap.finance. We will respond to your request within one month, as required by GDPR.

8. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). If such transfers occur, we ensure that adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data, such as the use of Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

9. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or destruction. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

10. Third-Party Service Providers

We may use third-party service providers to assist with our operations (e.g., hosting services, data analytics). These providers act as Data Processors on our behalf and are bound by contractual obligations to protect your data in compliance with GDPR standards.

11. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by GDPR.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through our platform or via email. Your continued use of the platform after such changes will constitute your acknowledgment and acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your GDPR rights, please contact us at team@dammcap.finance.
